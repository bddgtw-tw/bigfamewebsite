About Our Company

Taiwan B2B Sourcing, Supplier Matching & Export Coordination

Established in 1988, Big Fame IND. CORP. is a Taipei-based B2B sourcing and project coordination window for overseas clients buying retail display fixtures and merchandising hardware from Taiwan.

We identify suitable Taiwan manufacturers based on drawings, product categories, quantities, quality requirements and delivery schedules, then coordinate sampling, production, quality checks, consolidation and export.
