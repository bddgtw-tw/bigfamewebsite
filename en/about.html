ABOUT BIG FAME

About Our Company

Decades of Display Fixture & Retail Hardware Integration

Established in 1988, Big Fame IND. CORP. is a premier commercial display fixtures and retail merchandising metal hardware integration company headquartered in Taipei, Taiwan.

For nearly 40 years, we have served leading B2B retail markets across Japan, Europe, and North America. Our partnerships span diverse retail industries, including daily goods, apparel, sports lifestyle, eyewear accessories, and large retail chain stores.

OUR NETWORK

Synergy Across Three Locations

Taipei HQ

Engineering, Project Management, and Trade Coordination

Based in Taipei, Taiwan, this branch is the direct communication window for international clients. It handles CAD modeling, structure optimization, custom mold development, and export documentations.

Changhua Logistics

Consolidation, Quality Verification, and Logistics Control

Located near manufacturing clusters in central Taiwan, this facility manages secondary quality checks, coating testing, secure flat-packing, cargo consolidation, and container dispatching.

Malaysia Branch

ASEAN Resources Integration & Supply Chain Diversity

Serving as our expansion hub for Southeast Asia, this location monitors local production partners and coordinates supply backup lines, mitigating trade disruptions and geopolitical risks.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

Core Principles

"Quality in Detail, Flexibility in Execution."

Display hooks and metal fittings might seem minor, but we know their build quality—from welding cleanliness to anti-rust coating durability—determines the visual aesthetics of a premium store space.

To match the rapid adjustments of the retail market, we provide custom sampling, manufacture optimization, and mixed container cargo services, remaining a flexible, reliable, and efficient integration partner for your brand.
