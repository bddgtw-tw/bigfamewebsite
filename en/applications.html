APPLICATIONS

Global Retail Installations

【B2B Project Confidentiality Notice】

Big Fame maintains strict non-disclosure agreements (NDA) with global clothing brands, convenience store networks, and retail chains. To protect confidentiality, we do not present direct corporate logos or recognizable retail store photographs.

Below are 4 anonymized case studies highlighting our execution in material integration, structural engineering, and global logistics coordinates.

Case 01: Global Clothing Chain

Automated Welding Paint Adhesion Check 1,200+ Stores Global

Challenge: Large-scale metal gondola fixtures requiring burr-free robotic welding, high structural weight capacities, and durable powder-coating to resist friction.
Solution: Implemented robotic welding arms and conducted cross-hatch adhesion QC. Successfully deployed across 1,200+ stores, exporting 80+ TEU annually.

Case 02: Major Convenience Store Chain

SPCC Cold-Rolled Steel 120kg Load Test 5,000+ Outlets Japan

Challenge: Engineering ultra-slim metal shelves and heavy-duty seismic claws to pass strict commercial load inspections in Japan.
Solution: FEA optimization for claw stress, manufactured with precision SPCC steel bends. Safely integrated into over 5,000 retail store locations.

Case 03: European Eyewear Boutique Chain

Magnetic Security Lock 96H NSS Salt Spray Mirror Finish Electroplating

Challenge: Developing high-security magnetic display hooks for retail glasses, demanding pristine mirror-finish plating without cosmetic scratches.
Solution: Molded custom anti-theft magnetic detachers and validated finishes with 96-hour Neutral Salt Spray tests. Reduced retail store shrink rates below 8%.

Case 04: Multinational Hypermarket POS Displays

Tool-Free Quick Pin 45% Volume Reduction Global DDP Delivery

Challenge: Modular point-of-sale display hardware requiring assembly in under 3 minutes without tools, with minimum packaging volume.
Solution: Designed a tool-free quick-pin folding configuration, reducing packaging volume by 45% and lowering ocean logistics costs.
