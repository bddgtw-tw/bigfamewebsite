COMMON QUESTIONS

Planning a Taiwan sourcing project

How does Big Fame help overseas clients source from Taiwan?

We review drawings, product category, volume, quality requirements and destination, then help identify suitable Taiwan manufacturers and coordinate sampling, production, quality checks, consolidation and export.

How are sampling, production and export schedules confirmed?

Timing depends on the product category, process, volume, sample revisions and destination. After requirements are clear, we consolidate the sampling, production and export plan from suitable partners for review.

Can you handle international orders from overseas clients?

Yes. Big Fame acts as a Taiwan sourcing and export window, supporting English, Japanese and Chinese communication while coordinating manufacturers and export logistics.

What quality inspection standards do you follow?

We coordinate supplier inspection records and delivery checks against the approved specifications, including dimensions, appearance, packing contents and quantities where required.

Why work through a Taiwan-based sourcing partner?

For overseas clients, one sourcing window reduces the complexity of managing multiple Taiwan manufacturers, specifications, production schedules and export coordination.
