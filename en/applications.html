APPLICATIONS

Applications in Global Retail

[NDA & Confidentiality Statement]
Big Fame maintains strict Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs) with our international retail chain clients. To protect brand proprietary information and respect partnership guidelines, we do not directly display client logos, brand names, or identifiable on-site pictures. Below are generalized descriptions of where our display fittings are integrated.

Case 01: Global Apparel Brand

Robotic Welding Arm Coating Adhesion Test 1,200+ Stores Worldwide 80+ Containers/Year

Challenge: Burr-free precision welded display fixtures and apparel rail systems. High load-bearing capacity with durable coating that withstands years of friction. Core product hooks must endure hundreds of daily insertions and removals.
Solution: Automated robotic welding arms for burr-free production, with 100% cross-cut coating adhesion testing before shipment. Deployed across 1,200+ stores worldwide with 80+ containers shipped annually. 100% repeat order rate.

Case 02: Major Convenience Store Chain

SPCC Cold-Rolled Steel 120kg Load Test 5,000+ Stores in Japan Seismic Clip Design

Challenge: Develop ultra-thin yet high-load cold-rolled steel shelves with seismic clip structures. Must maintain compatibility with existing wall systems while passing the most stringent commercial load tests.
Solution: FEA-optimized stress distribution design for shelf clips, precision bending of SPCC cold-rolled steel. 3 prototype iterations before production. Deployed to 5,000+ stores across Japan with a defect rate below 0.1%.

Case 03: European Eyewear & Accessories Brand

Magnetic Security Lock 96H NSS Salt Spray Test Mirror-Grade Electroplating Loss Rate Below 8%

Challenge: Aesthetic security display hooks for eyewear. Mirror-grade electroplating finish with zero surface defects, while integrating anti-theft security mechanisms within the compact design.
Solution: Custom magnetic security release system developed in-house. Electroplated surfaces passed 96-hour neutral salt spray (NSS) testing for long-term appearance retention. Store loss rate reduced to below 8%.

Case 04: Global Retailer POS Promotional Displays

Tool-Free Assembly 45% Package Volume Reduction Global DDP Delivery Assembly Under 3 Min

Challenge: POS promotional displays that store staff can assemble in under 3 minutes without tools, with minimized shipping volume. Simultaneous delivery to multiple locations across Europe, North America, and Asia.
Solution: Quick-snap foldable structure design. Package size reduced by 45%, significantly lowering international shipping costs. Multi-hub consolidation and DDP delivery ensured on-time arrival at stores worldwide.
