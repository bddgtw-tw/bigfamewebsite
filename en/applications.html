APPLICATIONS

Applications in Global Retail

[NDA & Confidentiality Statement]
Big Fame maintains strict Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs) with our international retail chain clients. To protect brand proprietary information and respect partnership guidelines, we do not directly display client logos, brand names, or identifiable on-site pictures. Below are generalized descriptions of where our display fittings are integrated.

Mass Retail & Supermarkets

Heavy-duty display rack structures, custom wire baskets, and standard slatwall pegs. Engineered to withstand high-volume foot traffic and continuous merchandise replenishments.

Fashion & Apparel Boutiques

Custom hanger brackets, wall rails, and sleek clothing racks finished in matte black, chrome, or satin brass. Built with seamless weld joints to protect delicate fabrics and support brand image.

Eyewear & Accessories

Specialized security eyewear hang hooks, countertop display bases, and delicate acrylic-metal hybrid holders. Designed with high-precision bending and premium electro-plated finishes to highlight eyewear products.

Sports & Lifestyle Brands

High-strength metal brackets, adjustable shelving connectors, and modular pegboard attachments designed to secure heavy sporting goods or shoes, allowing flexible store configuration.

Established in 1988. A global integration partner for commercial display fixtures and retail merchandising hardware.
