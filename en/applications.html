Case 01: Global Apparel Brand

Robotic Welding Arm Coating Adhesion Test Multi-store deployment Multi-site delivery coordination

Challenge: Clean precision-welded display fixtures and apparel rail systems. High load-bearing capacity, durable coating and reliable repeated use were required.
Solution: Match the project with Taiwan partners that fit the required welding and finishing processes, then coordinate sampling, production follow-up, delivery checks and multi-site shipment.

Case 02: Major Convenience Store Chain

SPCC Cold-Rolled Steel High-load requirements Multi-store deployment Seismic Clip Design

Challenge: Develop ultra-thin yet high-load cold-rolled steel shelves with seismic clip structures. Must maintain compatibility with existing wall systems while passing the most stringent commercial load tests.
Solution: Match the load, wall-system and store-use requirements with suitable Taiwan sheet-metal and forming partners, then coordinate sample validation, specification approval and production delivery.

Case 03: European Eyewear & Accessories Brand

Magnetic Security Lock Finish verification Mirror-Grade Electroplating Security requirements

Challenge: Aesthetic security display hooks for eyewear, with consistent plating quality and anti-theft mechanisms integrated into a compact design.
Solution: Match the product and store context with partners that fit precision bending, finishing and security-accessory requirements, then coordinate appearance, mechanism and delivery specifications.

Case 04: Global Retailer POS Promotional Displays

Tool-Free Assembly Transport packaging optimization Multi-location export coordination Assembly Under 3 Min

Challenge: POS promotional displays requiring practical assembly, efficient transport packaging and delivery to multiple locations.
Solution: Match the structure and distribution requirements with suitable Taiwan partners, then coordinate quick-assembly design, packaging optimization, consolidation and export delivery.
