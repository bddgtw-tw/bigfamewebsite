CAPABILITIES & PRODUCTS

Capabilities & Products

01 / FIXTURES

Commercial Display Fixtures

Modular wall fixtures, gondolas, clothing display racks, and freestanding display structures used in brand retail stores, fashion boutiques, and supermarkets.

  • Steel, stainless steel, and brass pipe/sheet custom fabrications
  • Multi-material assemblies incorporating wood, acrylics, and integrated LEDs
  • Premium finishes: powder coatings, satin chrome plating, and brushed brass
02 / HOOKS & ACCESSORIES

Display Hooks & Wall Accessories

An array of retail merchandising hooks designed to attach seamlessly to standard wall panels or metal tube frames. Built with tight dimensional tolerances for smooth retail setup.

  • Merchandising hooks for pegboards, slotwalls, and rectangular crossbars
  • Anti-theft secure hooks with magnetic locks
  • Specialty curved eyewear and sunglass loops
  • Metal brackets, scanner rail holders, and card signholders
03 / PROMOTIONS

POS & POP Displays

Point-of-sale display systems designed for cash counters and promotional aisles to capture consumer attention. Optimized for space-saving packing and easy store assembly.

  • Countertop cosmetic displays and drugstore branding trays
  • Wire-mesh baskets, retail dump bins, and wire grid stands
  • Integrated branding holders and tool-free snap-fitting structures
04 / CUSTOM COMPONENTS

Custom Metal Components

We offer B2B OEM/ODM manufacturing services for custom metal fittings, hinges, joints, and specific hardware components based on client engineering drawings.

  • Precision stamping, metal casting, and tube bending for steel and zinc alloys
  • Robot-arm welding and high-speed CNC metal machining
  • Durable electro-plating, chrome plating, and eco-friendly powder finishes
WORKING WITH US

MOQ & Production Lead Time

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

As a project-based B2B integration manufacturer, we maintain flexible order policies to accommodate prototyping and full-scale retail rollouts:

  • Custom Metal Hardware Items: Typically 500+ pieces per item.
  • Complete Store Display Racks: Typically 50+ sets (trial orders and pilot store rollouts welcome).
  • Standard Parts / Existing Molds: Smaller quantities package-based, please check with our PM team.

Typical Lead Time

We strictly control project timelines to guarantee on-time retail store openings:

  • Sampling & Prototyping: Approximately 2 to 3 weeks after drawing sign-off.
  • Initial Production Run: Approximately 6 to 8 weeks after sample approval and tooling set-up.
  • Repeat Orders: Typically completed in 4 to 6 weeks, depending on materials buffer and scheduling.
