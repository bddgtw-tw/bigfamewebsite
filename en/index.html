OUR FOCUS

Our Core Positioning

What we manufacture is not standard furniture, nor is it simple shelving. We deliver commercial display fixtures and retail merchandising hardware integrated systems—essential elements that showcase products, project brand identity, and optimize customer flow within global physical retail spaces.

OUR PROCESS

Our End-to-End Service Journey

Step 01

Consultation & Engineering Optimization

We work closely with client design teams to translate creative retail concepts into production-ready engineering drawings optimized for cost, structure, and strength.

Step 02

Tooling Development & Rapid Prototyping

Led by our Taipei team, we develop metal molds and deliver high-fidelity prototypes quickly for review, assembly testing, and finish approval.

Step 03

Rigorous Inspection & Batch Production

We carry out in-line inspections during manufacturing and final strict quality control at our Changhua hub, including paint adhesion and high-load capacity tests.

Step 04

Custom Packaging & Global Logistics

We pack items specifically for retail distribution (individual store kits or warehouse bulk), optimizing package safety, and schedule container consolidation for worldwide shipment.
