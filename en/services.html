OUR SERVICES

End-to-End Service Workflow

Big Fame acts as your dedicated B2B procurement coordinator in Taiwan and Southeast Asia, streamlining the entire path from design drafting to on-site store delivery.

01

Specification Evaluation & Value Engineering (VE)

We align with your design agency or sourcing team to review blueprints. Our Taipei engineers analyze tolerances, load limitations, and surface treatments, offering VE proposals to optimize production costs without sacrificing strength.

02

CAD Optimization & Fast Prototyping

Upon drawing approval, we rapidly manufacture physical samples. This allows your team to verify structural clearances, welding aesthetics, plating thickness, and assembly mechanisms before signing off for mass fabrication.

03

Sourcing Coordination & Manufacturing Integration

We match different components (tube extrusion, wire bending, sheet stamping, wood parts) to specialized partner facilities. Taipei HQ manages production milestones and supply chain schedules to ensure timeline compliance.

04

Double Quality Verification (Quality Control)

Completed items undergo final audits at our Changhua logistics center. We conduct systematic assembly testing, coating adhesion grid tests, salt-spray corrosion tests, and component count verification against strict QC lists.

05

Transit Packaging & Custom Labeling

To protect metal surfaces during long-distance shipping, we utilize heavy-duty double-wall cartons, custom foam molds, and protective films. Clear barcoded carton labels and multi-language instructions are included to aid store staff.

06

Customs clearance & Global Mixed Cargo Logistics

We coordinate ocean and air shipments to designated ports globally. We consolidate cargo from different manufacturing lines, manage export customs declarations, and arrange localized warehousing or DDP final delivery.
