WORKFLOW

Our 6-Step Project Flow

Managing Taipei HQ PM & engineering, Changhua QC inspect hubs, and Malaysia supply lines backup for seamless retail execution.

01

Requirement Scoping & Value Engineering

We collaborate with client design and procurement teams to clarify load capacities, paint gloss, and chemical resistance. Our Taipei team offers "Value Engineering (VE)" advice on tooling and hardware structures, lowering production costs without sacrificing strength or brand aesthetics.

02

Design Engineering & Prototyping

Based on finalized blueprints, our skilled craftspeople manufacture high-precision physical samples. This allows clients to test assembly tolerances, weld finishes, and powder coating texture, making micro-adjustments before mass production.

03

Manufacturing Integration & Progress Tracking

Products are routed to specialized facilities based on fabrication process (extrusion, stamping, die-casting). Taipei PMs control progress across all component plants to ensure on-time assembly aggregation.

04

Secondary Quality Control at Logistical Warehouses

All manufactured batch outputs are sent to our self-owned warehouse hub in Changhua, Taiwan, for secondary quality inspections, including trial assemblies, salt-spray tests, and component audits.

05

Industrial Packing & Multilingual Instructions

To withstand long ocean shipping journeys, we use reinforced boxes and edge protectors. We coordinate cross-factory consolidation, manage customs clearance, and collaborate with global freight forwarders to deliver to your stores or warehouses.

06

Consolidation & Global DDP Logistics

We arrange factory-wide consolidation, container packing coordinates, export customs declaration, and delivery to designated ports or warehouses globally.
