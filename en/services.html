OUR SERVICES

End-to-End Service Workflow

Big Fame acts as your dedicated B2B procurement coordinator in Taiwan. For metal display equipment, or project-based island fixtures and store fixtures combining wood, acrylics and lighting, we find suitable partners and coordinate the path from requirements to export delivery.

01

Specification Evaluation & Value Engineering (VE)

We align with your design agency or sourcing team to review blueprints. Our Taipei engineers analyze tolerances, load limitations, and surface treatments, offering VE proposals to optimize production costs without sacrificing strength.

02

Manufacturer Matching & Fast Prototyping

After the requirements are clear, we identify a suitable Taiwan partner and coordinate physical samples so your team can verify clearances, finishing and assembly before production approval.

03

Sourcing Coordination & Manufacturing Integration

We match metal, wood, acrylic and lighting components to suitable specialist partners. Taipei HQ manages production milestones and supply chain schedules so island displays, store fixtures and mixed-material assemblies can be consolidated for delivery.

04

Delivery Quality Coordination (Quality Control)

We coordinate supplier inspection records and delivery checks against the approved specifications, including dimensions, appearance, packing contents and quantities where required.

05

Transit Packaging & Custom Labeling

To protect metal surfaces during long-distance shipping, we utilize heavy-duty double-wall cartons, custom foam molds, and protective films. Clear barcoded carton labels and multi-language instructions are included to aid store staff.

06

Customs clearance & Global Mixed Cargo Logistics

We coordinate ocean and air shipments to designated ports. We consolidate cargo from different manufacturing lines, manage export customs declarations, and coordinate destination delivery requirements.
